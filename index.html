Context and references

Performance estimation problems were introduced in 2014 by Yoel Drori and Marc Teboulle. This mini-course mainly follows the perspective developed by Adrien Taylor, Julien Hendrickx, and Francois Glineur, with an accessible informal introduction in Francis Bach's blog post on computer-aided analyses.

  • [1] Performance of first-order methods for smooth convex minimization: a novel approach
  • [2] Smooth strongly convex interpolation and exact worst-case performance of first-order methods
  • [3] Exact worst-case performance of first-order methods for composite convex optimization
  • Informal introduction: Computer-aided analyses
