Context and references

Performance estimation problems were introduced in [1]. This mini-course mainly follows the framework and developments from [2-4], together with the informal introduction in [5].

The labs are based on the PEPit code package for performance estimation problems [4].

  • [1] Performance of first-order methods for smooth convex minimization: a novel approach
  • [2] Smooth strongly convex interpolation and exact worst-case performance of first-order methods
  • [3] Exact worst-case performance of first-order methods for composite convex optimization
  • [4] B. Goujaud, C. Moucer, F. Glineur, J. M. Hendrickx, A. B. Taylor, and A. Dieuleveut. PEPit: computer-assisted worst-case analyses of first-order optimization methods in Python Mathematical Programming Computation 16(3), 337-367.
  • [5] Computer-aided analyses
